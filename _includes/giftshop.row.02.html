Baron Von Steuben's Manuel of Arms Mementos

A full color rendition of an original recruiting poster illustrating drilling positions from Baron Friedrich Wilhelm von Steuben's Regulations for the Order and Discipline of the Troops of the United States. This drill manual, originally developed in 1778 at Valley Forge for the training of American infantry, was the official US military guide until 1812.
