The 1887 White Plains, NY Aerial Lithograph Large black and white aerial map of the Village of White Plains, NY in 1887, originally published by L. R. Burleigh Litho Establishment of Troy, NY. Shows many old landmarks including the third County Courthouse and Jail, churches, homes, and businesses, street names, the original train depot, and the hilly farmland surrounding the Village. This print is reproduced on thick, high quality paper and is a MUST for any home or office.
