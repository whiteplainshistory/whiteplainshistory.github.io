Bendheim Pavilion (1938), White Plains Hospital, 41 East Post Road.
One of two sculptures over the front entrance. Sculpture is by Rene Paul Chambellan,
who also designed the Daily News Building in New York,
 many of the fountains at Rockefeller Center,
and the Atlas statue in Rockefeller Center (designed with Lee Lawrie).
