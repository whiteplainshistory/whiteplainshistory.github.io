Barry Katzen has lived in White Plains since 1981. He has worked in publishing houses in New York City and Westchester, and is currently senior online marketing manager at Pearson Longman in White Plains. He has a Bachelor of Arts from Binghamton University (formerly SUNY-Binghamton) and a Master's of Arts from the University of Wisconsin.

Barry is a published photographer (Southwest Silver Jewelry, 2001).
