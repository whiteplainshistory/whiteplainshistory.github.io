May 4, 2006 - White Plains, NY.

He is a Senior Editor at National Review and a political columnist for The New York Observer. He has also contributed to American Heritage, The Atlantic Monthly, The New Yorker, The New York Times Book Review, Time, Vanity Fair, and many others.
